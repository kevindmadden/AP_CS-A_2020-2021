Project #04 - Animation Challenge

Requirements:

  1. Choose two animations from piazza that have been deemed "doable."
  2. Successfully implement these two animations.
  3. You must challenge yourself: For at least one of the animations that you implement, choose one of the doable options that you do not immediately see a way to do.

Some tips:

  • Break up the animations into simpler steps such that you are able to test each step along the way.
  • Take breaks. Let your brain work through the problem while you aren't actively working on it.
  • Ask for help when you need it. There's nothing to be ashamed of in asking for help. It likely just means that you are doing this assignment correctly.

Reminder:

  • The purpose of the assignment is to challenge yourself.
